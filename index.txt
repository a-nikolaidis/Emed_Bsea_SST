We are providing reconstructed sea surface temperature (SST) satellite images and data, covering the Eastern Mediterranean and Black Sea regions.

.. figure:: /images/sample_SST__23.jpg
   :target: /images/sample_SST__23.jpg?zz=1
   :class: thumbnail
   :alt: reconstucted satellite SST


Cloud-free data were produced by applying the `DINEOF <http://modb.oce.ulg.ac.be/mediawiki/index.php/DINEOF>`__ algorithm over the `EUMEDSAT <http://www.osi-saf.org/>`__ five (5) km grid Level 3 data on daily basis, for a seven (7) days period of hourly steps.
